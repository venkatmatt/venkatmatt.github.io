Memo & Blob View

When a table has Memo or Blob/Picture fields, they are automatically displayed at Memo/Blob panel, below the data grid.

Open the Categories table by clicking the checkbox to its right.

Note a panel containing two tabs Description and Picture is displayed below the grid.

The Description is a Memo field (i.e multi-line text)



The Picture tab shows picture from the Picture field.
