Columns Customize

Columns Customize feature allows you to hide unwanted columns and re-arrange their order. This is especially useful when you want to export data in a particular format.


  1. Open the Customers table and click on the  Customize Columns button on the Grid Toolbar. The Column Customize popup (illustrated to the right) is displayed.
  2. Unselect columns ContactName, ContactTitle, Address, PostalCode, Fax.
  3. Drag column Country and drop just below CompanyName, so that it appears next to it.
  4. Drag column Region and drop it just below the Counrtry.
  5. Click the Save and Close  button on the Title Bar of the popup.


The grid now displays only the selected columns, in their new order
