Auto Search

The Auto Search feature enable you to quickly locate a record in the Data Grid by entering the first few characters of it value. Auto Search works only when the Auto Search Option of the Grid Menu is be enabled.


To Search for a record

  • If you are editing data save or cancel and come out of editing mode.
  • Click on the cell whose value you want to search for.
  • Enter the first few characters of its value you want to search for - as you enter the characters the record pointer will move to the matched record highlighting the matched characters. You can use the Backspace key to edit the characters you have entered.
