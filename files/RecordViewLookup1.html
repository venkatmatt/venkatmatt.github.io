Record View & Lookup

DbSee can also show Many-to-One related data in Record-View.


  1. Open the Products table by clicking the checkbox to its right
  2. On the Data area of Products click the Lookup menu and select Suppliers




Record View panel is displayed with two tabs - one for the Products which displays the current record of the grid and the other for the Suppliers which displays the matching record from the Suppliers. You Check / Uncheck any item in the Lookup menu to show / hide the Lookup Record View.

The Record View Panel itself can be Hidden or Shown by clicking the button on the Grid Toolbar.
