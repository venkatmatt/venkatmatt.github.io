User Interface

DbSee has simple user interface. When launched its Main screen is displayed maximized.


The Main Screen has

  • A Toolbar at the top with 6 buttons/menu items.
  • A Status Bar at the bottom.
  • Meta-data display area at the left consisting of 3 Tab pages: Queries, Tables and Views.
  • Data Display area at the right where the Query, Table or View data is displayed in grid, under separate Tab Pages.

Almost all visual controls have a Tool tip hint, briefly describing their function. Just hover the mouse over the control to display its hint.

DbSee uses Vertical and Horizontal Splitters that can be used

    • to adjust the size of display area by dragging.        
    • to minimize / restore display areas by clicking on the grip displayed at the center of the splitter.        

The bottom panel in the Meta Data tabs that displays Columns information or SQL Query can also be resized by dragging its top edge.
